The Best Movies According To Joseph

My top 3 movies of all time.

The Perks Of Being A Wallflower

I really love this movie because the quote.
"We accept the love we think we deserve."

Catch Me If You Can

Good movie about a con artist. I like the cat and mouse game between the main characters.

Mr. & Mrs. Smith

This movie is really fun to watch. I like the action and the comedy. Brad Pitt and Angelina Jolie have great chemistry.
